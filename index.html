Let's Play This Awesome Crystal Game!

Instructions:

You will be given a random number at the beginning of the game.

There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal, you will add a sepcific number of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to the random number (Hooray!!). You lose the game if your sore goes above the random number (Boo! You suck!!).

But there's a catch! The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it. Each time the game restarts, the values of the crystals will change.

Good luck, and don't blow it!
